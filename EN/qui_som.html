VOIDCORE

We are Voidcore, a small indie company from Barcelona founded by Chafik and Noèlia. We're passionate about creating unique video games, blending a retrofuturistic style with fun, lively mechanics!


At Voidcore, we aim to transport you to different worlds with immersive experiences you won't forget. If you're looking for something new and special, you're in the right place!


Our first video game, The Emptiness Machine, is now available! An adventure full of personality that’s just the beginning of a journey we want to share with you!
