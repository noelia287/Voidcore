The Story

Waking up aboard the HORIZON ship, you find yourself racing against time to destroy a robotic arm trying to implant you with a control chip. ARKAN, the evil AI that controls the ship, has begun a sinister trade using humans as living bioreactors.

Your Mission

As the last uninfected survivor, you must:

  • Navigate through sealed chambers and space voids
  • Decode ARKAN's hidden protocols
  • Destroy biomechanical control systems
  • Uncover the fate of the human reactors

Every step you take could be your last... The ship is watching you!
